# DRAFTI – OFFICIAL GAME RULES & PLATFORM POLICY

## 1. INTRODUCTION
Drafti is a competitive online draughts (checkers) platform built around Tanzanian draught principles, skill-based matchmaking, and community-focused competition. This policy defines the official rules of play, ranking systems, matchmaking logic, and player conduct standards applicable to all Drafti users.

By creating an account or playing Drafti, users agree to abide by this policy.

---

## 2. GAME RULES

### 2.1 Board & Setup
- Board size: 8 × 8 squares
- Each player starts with 12 pieces
- Pieces occupy dark squares only
- Players are assigned Red or Black

### 2.2 Piece Movement
- Normal pieces move one square diagonally forward
- Backward movement is not allowed for normal pieces

### 2.3 Mandatory Captures
- Capturing is mandatory
- If a capture is available, the player must take it
- Multiple captures in a single turn are required when possible
- The turn ends only when no further capture is available

### 2.4 King Promotion
- A piece becomes a King upon reaching the opponent’s final row
- Kings may move and capture diagonally forward and backward

### 2.5 Winning Conditions
A game is won when:
- The opponent has no remaining pieces
- The opponent has no legal moves
- The opponent resigns
- The opponent runs out of time
- The opponent disconnects beyond the allowed grace period

### 2.6 Draw Conditions
A game is declared a draw when:
- The same board position occurs three times
- No capture occurs for 40 consecutive moves
- Both players mutually agree to a draw

- Used for rankings, leaderboards, and competitive progression

## 4. MATCHMAKING POLICY

### 4.1 Local-First Matchmaking
Drafti prioritizes fair and local competition. Matchmaking follows an expanding search model:

1. Same region, ILO ±100
2. Same country, ILO ±150
3. Neighboring countries, ILO ±200
4. Global matchmaking, ILO ±300

Search parameters expand automatically based on wait time.

### 4.2 Time Controls
- Blitz: 5 minutes
- Rapid: 10 minutes
- Classic: 15 minutes or more

---

## 5. ILO RATING SYSTEM

### 5.1 Starting Rating
- All new players start at 1000 ILO

### 5.2 Rating Adjustments
ILO changes are calculated based on game outcome and opponent strength:

- Win vs stronger opponent: +30 to +35
- Win vs equal opponent: +20
- Win vs weaker opponent: +10 to +15
- Loss vs stronger opponent: −10
- Loss vs equal opponent: −20
- Loss vs weaker opponent: −25 to −30
- Draw: −10 to +10

### 5.3 Anti-Abuse Measures
- Repeated matches against the same opponent reduce ILO gains
- Disconnects are recorded as losses
- Suspicious behavior is automatically flagged

## 8. FAIR PLAY POLICY

Players must not:
- Use bots, scripts, or external engines
- Manipulate ratings through intentional losses
- Exploit disconnects to avoid losses
- Harass or abuse other players

Violations may result in:
- Rating adjustment
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent account ban
