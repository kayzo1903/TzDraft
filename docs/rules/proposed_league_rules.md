# Proposed Basic League Tournament Rules

These are the simple rules for the TzDraft League system. Please review and revise them. Once approved, I will create dedicated pages to explain various scenarios.

## 1. Match Format
- Each league match consists of **two (2) games**.
- Players alternate playing as White and Black.

## 2. Scoring System
- **Match Win**: 3 points (awarded if a player wins more games or wins on aggregate).
- **Match Draw**: 1 point (awarded if each player wins one game or both games are draws).
- **Match Loss**: 0 points.

## 3. Game Styles (Time Control)
Each league session will specify its style:
- **Blitz**: Fast-paced games (e.g., 3-5 minutes).
- **Rapid**: Standard tournament speed (e.g., 10-15 minutes).
- **Classic**: Longer, strategic games (e.g., 30+ minutes).
- Increments may be applied based on the specific league setup.

## 4. Game Rules
- All matches follow the **Official TZD (Tanzania Draughts) Rules**.
- This includes specific interpretations of mandatory captures, king movements, and draw conditions (like the 30-move and Three-kings rules).

## 5. Ranking and Tie-breaking
- Rankings are determined by total points.
- Tie-breakers:
  1. Head-to-head result.
  2. Total games won.
  3. Average ELO of opponents.

# Mapendekezo ya Sheria za Ligi ya Rasimu (Kiswahili)

Hizi ni sheria rahisi za mfumo wa Ligi ya TzDraft. Tafadhali nirekebishe pale inapobidi kabla hatujaandaa kurasa maalum za kuelezea matukio mbalimbali.

## 1. Mfumo wa Mechi
- Kila mechi ya ligi itakuwa na **michezo miwili (2)**.
- Wachezaji watabadilishana kuanza (mmoja mweupe, mwingine mweusi).

## 2. Mfumo wa Alama (Pointi)
- **Ushindi wa Mechi**: Alama 3.
- **Sare ya Mechi**: Alama 1.
- **Kupoteza Mechi**: Alama 0.

## 3. Mitindo ya Mchezo (Muda)
Kila ligi itakuwa na mtindo wake wa muda:
- **Blitz**: Michezo ya haraka sana (mfano, dakika 3-5).
- **Rapid**: Kasi ya kawaida ya mashindano (mfano, dakika 10-15).
- **Classic**: Michezo mirefu ya kimkakati (mfano, dakika 30 au zaidi).
- Nyongeza ya muda (Increments) inaweza kuongezwa kulingana na mpangilio wa ligi.

## 4. Sheria za Mchezo
- Mechi zote zinafuata **Sheria Rasmi za TZD (Tanzania Draughts)**.
- Hii inajumuisha sheria za lazima za kukamata, mienendo ya wafalme, na vigezo vya sare (kama sheria ya hatua 30 na wafalme watatu).

## 5. Msimamo na Vigezo vya Sare
- Msimamo utaamuliwa kwa jumla ya alama.
- Vigezo vya ziada (Tie-breakers):
  1. Matokeo ya mechi baina ya wachezaji waliofungana (Head-to-head).
  2. Idadi ya michezo iliyoshinda.
  3. Wastani wa ELO wa wapinzani.
