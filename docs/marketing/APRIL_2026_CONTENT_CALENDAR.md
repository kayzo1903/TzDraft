# TzDraft April 2026 Content Calendar

**Date range:** 2026-04-01 to 2026-04-30  
**Timezone:** East Africa Time (EAT)  
**Audience:** Solo founder / small marketing team  
**Related documents:** `docs/marketing/TZDRAFT_CONTENT_STRATEGY.md`, `docs/marketing/SOLO_LOW_COST_PROMOTION_PLAN.md`

---

## 1. Calendar Goal

This calendar turns the TzDraft content strategy into a daily publishing plan for **April 2026**.

It is designed to:

1. Keep one clear content rhythm every week
2. Push players toward AI, friend matches, tournaments, and learning pages
3. Reuse tournament activity as low-cost content
4. Stay realistic for one person managing both product and promotion

---

## 2. Publishing Rhythm

Use these default posting windows unless real audience data shows a better time:

- **Education posts:** 1:00 PM EAT
- **Challenge and rivalry posts:** 7:00 PM EAT
- **Tournament announcements:** 7:30 PM EAT
- **Friday reminders:** 6:00 PM EAT
- **Saturday live updates:** 7:30 PM EAT and 8:30 PM EAT
- **Sunday recaps:** 5:00 PM EAT

---

## 3. Channel Roles

- **Facebook:** main public post and community sharing channel
- **WhatsApp:** reminder and conversion channel for warm players
- **YouTube Shorts:** short-form discovery for gameplay, puzzles, and rules
- **Onsite `/learn` or `/rules`:** evergreen content hub for SEO and trust

---

## 4. Weekly Themes

- **Week 1: Kickoff and awareness**
- **Week 2: Education and beginner activation**
- **Week 3: Community proof and competition**
- **Week 4: Training and credibility**
- **Week 5: Monthly wrap-up and May momentum**

## 6. Minimum Weekly Deliverables

If time gets tight, protect these first:

1. One Monday article or rules post
2. One Tuesday AI challenge
3. One Thursday tournament announcement
4. One Friday reminder
5. One Sunday recap

Everything else is a bonus, but these five keep the growth loop alive.

---

## 7. Asset Checklist

Prepare these reusable assets once, then repeat them:

- Tournament poster template
- Winner recap template
- AI challenge card template
- Puzzle image template
- Leaderboard update template
- Simple screen-recording template for friend-match walkthroughs

---

## 8. Review Checklist

At the end of each week, record:

1. Which post got the most clicks
2. Which CTA drove the most sessions
3. Which post format was easiest to produce
4. How many registrations came from WhatsApp versus Facebook
5. Which topic should be repeated next week

This turns the calendar from a posting plan into a learning system.
