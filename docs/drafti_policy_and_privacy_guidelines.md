# Drafti – Policy and Privacy Guidelines

## 1. Purpose
This document establishes the **official policy, privacy, and security guidelines** for the Drafti platform.

It ensures:
- Compliance with **data protection best practices**
- Protection of **player privacy and identity**
- Transparent **rules and user expectations**
- Safe, fair, and ethical gameplay

---

## 2. User Account Policy

1. **Eligibility**
   - Users must be at least 13 years old.
   - Each user can register only one account.

2. **Account Creation**
   - Users provide a valid username, display name, and password.
   - Email or phone verification may be required for account activation.

3. **Account Security**
   - Passwords must be stored securely (hashed + salted).
   - Users are responsible for maintaining account confidentiality.
   - Suspicious activity may result in temporary suspension.

---

## 3. Gameplay Policy

1. **Fair Play**
   - All games are **server-authoritative**.
   - Cheating, exploitation, or use of unauthorized engines is strictly prohibited.
   - Multi-accounting to gain unfair advantage is prohibited.

2. **Matchmaking**
   - Players are matched based on ELO rating and game type.
   - AI games must use designated AI levels (350–2500).

3. **Abuse and Misconduct**
   - Harassment, offensive messages, or disruptive behavior may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent bans.

---

## 4. Privacy Guidelines

1. **Data Collection**
   - Personal identifiers: username, email (optional), registration timestamp.
   - Gameplay data: moves, game results, timestamps, AI levels.
   - Device information: IP address, client version, optional analytics.

2. **Data Usage**
   - Improve gameplay experience and matchmaking.
   - Detect cheating or fraud.
   - Maintain leaderboards and performance statistics.

3. **Data Sharing**
   - No personal data is shared with third parties without consent.
   - Aggregated, anonymized statistics may be used for analytics or research.

4. **User Rights**
   - Access: Users can request account and gameplay data.
   - Deletion: Users can request account deletion; data is anonymized in logs for compliance.
   - Correction: Users can update or correct personal information.

---

## 5. Security Measures

1. **Server-Side Authority**
   - All moves, clocks, and game results are verified and stored server-side.
   - Clients never have direct authority over game outcome.

2. **Encryption**
   - All network communication uses HTTPS/WSS (TLS encryption).
   - Passwords and sensitive data are securely encrypted.

3. **Auditing**
   - All moves and game actions are logged immutably for dispute resolution.

4. **Incident Response**
   - Suspicious activity triggers alerts.
   - Breaches or data incidents will be reported according to applicable regulations.

---

## 6. AI Usage Policy

- AI opponents are restricted to **pre-defined levels** (350–2500).
- Engine integration is fully **server-authoritative**.
- Users cannot use external engines to manipulate gameplay outcomes.

---

## 7. Realtime & Spectator Policy

- Spectators receive **read-only game states**.
- No private or personal data is exposed through the spectator view.
- Spectator activity is logged to prevent abuse.

---

## 8. Compliance & Legal Notes

- Policies comply with **general data protection principles**.
- Users are informed of terms upon registration.
- Policy may be updated; users are notified in-app or via email.

---

## 9. Enforcement

- Violations of policy may result in:
  - Warnings
  - Temporary suspension
  - Permanent ban
- Abuse, cheating, or illegal activity may also lead to **legal action** if applicable.

---

## 10. Summary

Drafti is committed to:

- **Fair, transparent gameplay**  
- **User privacy and data protection**  
- **Server-authoritative rules enforcement**  
- **Ethical AI integration**  

> Players can enjoy Drafti knowing their data is safe, gameplay is fair, and privacy is respected.
